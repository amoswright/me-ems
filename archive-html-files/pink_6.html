For ET size, pinky finger diameter in a child affords an acceptable approximate of ET tube outer diameter. The formula for tube size is as follows: "Age (in years)/4 + 3.5 (cuffed)" and the length-based tape may be used for internal diameter determination. Using a tube one size larger or smaller than this guideline is also acceptable.
