Spine Assessment & Management #1

This protocol is for suspected spinal injury, based on the complaint and mechanism of injury*

graph TD A[Does the patient have any of the following findings: * Unreliable?** * Distracting injury?*** * Abnormal sensory or motor exam? * Spine pain or tenderness?] --> B[Do not immobilize] A -- Yes --> C{Does the patient have a helmet on?} C -- Yes --> D[Manually remove if clinically indicated (for airway management, hemorrhage control, etc.), if possible] C -- No --> E{Is the patient in a seated position and able to self-extricate?****} E -- Yes --> F[Place in cervical collar and allow patient to self-extricate] E -- No --> G[Immobilize cervical spine with collar or alternate method to restrict spinal motion -THEN- Extricate with backboard OR other extrication device -OR- If child is in car seat, extricate in the car seat if child is already strapped in with harness] F --> H[Once patient is on the EMS stretcher, remove the backboard UNLESS safe transport requires the use of a backboard (see PEARLS). Transfer patient to appropriate hospital based on trauma triage decisions. If patient is in a car seat, see Pediatric Transportation protocol, Pink 7.] G --> H
