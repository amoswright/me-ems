ADVANCED EMT

  1. If a patient's condition necessitates IV placement for another reason AND the oral route of acetaminophen is contraindicated, AND the patient is 70kg or greater, consider acetaminophen 1000 mg (1 gram) IV once, over 15 minutes.

PARAMEDIC

  1. If a patient's condition necessitates IV placement for another reason, AND the oral route of acetaminophen is contraindicated, AND the patient is greater than 2 years old, consider acetaminophen 10-15 mg/kg IV once up to a MAX of 1000 mg, over 15 minutes.
    a. NOTE: doses of acetaminophen less than 1000 mg require infusion via pump.

PEARLS

  • An IV should not be placed solely for the administration of IV acetaminophen.
  • The administration of IV administration should not interfere with the resuscitation of the patient (e.g., fluid resuscitation)
