Adult Post-Resuscitation Care Checklist

SAVE A LIFE
S Stabilize - Stay on scene vs. rapid departure
A Airway - if still unresponsive, establish a definitive airway (SGA, ETT)
V Vitals - change frequency to every 3 minutes
Vasopressors (NOREPIneprine) - focus on cerebral perfusion pressure, goal is MAP greater than 80 mmHg
E EtCO₂ - monitor airway and for re-arrest
A Alert/activate hospital
L Leave/Load in ambulance with mechanical CPR device in place, if available in case patient rearrests en route to hospital
I IV - if only an IO has been placed
F Follow-up vital signs to ensure stability before departure
E ECG - may affect destination, delayed ECG increases accuracy
