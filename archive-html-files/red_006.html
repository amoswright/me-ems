Chest Pain Fibrinolytic Checklist

For chest pain of suspected cardiac origin, initiate therapy per protocol, including the early use of aspirin and nitroglycerin, if not contraindicated.

Use this checklist, or local equivalent, if available.

Report the information as soon as practical to the receiving ED.

You may copy and use this page as your checklist, or you may use a checklist recommended by your usual receiving hospital which contains at least these questions.
