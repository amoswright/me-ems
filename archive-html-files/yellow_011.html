Treatment

EMT

  1. Prevent further heat loss by insulating from the ground and shielding from wind and water. Move to a warm environment, when possible. Gently remove wet clothing. Cover with warm blankets.
  2. Pack thorax with wrapped heat packs (heat packs cannot exceed 113°F); if available, may use hospital-grade electric forced air re-warmers.
  3. Consider warmed AND humidified 100% O₂.
  4. High sugar oral fluids, if tolerated, and only in mild hypothermia.
  5. Handle gently; avoid rough movement and excess activity.
  6. Maintain supine position in moderate or severe hypothermia.
  7. Apply clean dressing to frostbitten extremities and between involved fingers and toes.
