1. Contact OLMC before using any analgesic (beyond acetaminophen) in:
    1. Isolated trauma involving the head,
    2. Back pain,
    3. Non-traumatic extremity pain,
    4. Any patient with mental status changes,
    5. Any patient with unstable vital signs (including SBP < 100 mmHg),
    6. Coincident drug use (including alcohol).
