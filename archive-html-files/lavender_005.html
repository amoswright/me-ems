Pearls:

  1. Remember to obtain APGAR scores at 1 and 5 minutes on baby, see Lavender 4
  2. NEVER GIVE NALOXONE TO A NEONATE
  3. In neonates, ventilation and oxygenation may increase heart rate in properly placed advanced airways sooner than chest compressions.
  4. In a newborn, the SpO₂ monitor should be placed on the RIGHT hand
  5. In a newborn, goal Peak Inspiratory Pressure should be 20 cmH₂O with 5 cmH₂O of PEEP (Paramedic level, if certified by NRP, or equivalent training, credentialed by their service-level medical director, and properly equipped).
  6. In a newborn, use 3:1 compression-to-ventilation ratio with a pause for ventilation, even with a BIAD or ETT
